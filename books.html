Featured Book

I would like to highlight the book Provenance and Data Management for Scientific Workflows, co-authored by Daniel de Oliveira, Ji Liu, and Esther Pacitti. This work provides a thorough exploration of managing data-intensive workflows in scientific research, focusing on reproducibility, transparency, and efficiency.

Key Topics Covered

  • Workflow management principles and best practices
  • Workflow Execution in a Single-Site Cloud
  • Workflow Execution in a Multi-Site Cloud
  • Real-world case studies in bioinformatics, astronomy, and engineering
  • Workflow Execution in DISC Environments

This book is a valuable resource for researchers, data scientists, and professionals interested in practical and theoretical insights on scientific workflow management.
